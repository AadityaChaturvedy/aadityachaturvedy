## 🧠 About Me

> *"The best research doesn't just answer questions — it reframes them."*

I'm a researcher and engineer operating at the intersection of **multimodal AI**, **biomedical systems**, and **intelligent hardware**. My work spans from designing novel grounding mechanisms in Vision-Language Models to deploying sensor-fused IoT platforms for real-world agricultural intelligence. I build systems that are not just technically sound — they are designed to matter.

- 🔬 Actively researching **Vision-Language Models**, **medical image analysis**, and **efficient inference** (quantized, edge-deployable models)
- 🌾 Building **IoT + ML pipelines** that bridge physical sensors with satellite-scale data
- 🔐 Grounded in **cryptography**, **secure systems design**, and low-level algorithmic thinking
- 🎨 Exploring **3D modeling** in Blender as a creative counterbalance to research
- 🤝 Open to **high-impact collaborations** in biomedical AI, multimodal learning, and applied ML research
